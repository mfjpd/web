BIG 3 Special Menu

Discover our three special pizzas, crafted to delight every palate with unique flavors and fresh ingredients.

Mediterranean Marvel

This pizza is loaded with juicy Kalamata olives, artichoke hearts, feta cheese, and sun-dried tomatoes. The combination of salty, sweet, and tangy flavors creates a perfect harmony that will transport your taste buds to the Mediterranean coast!

Spicy Italian Stallion

For those who like a little heat, this pizza is topped with spicy Italian sausage, caramelized onions, mushrooms, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes. The spicy kick from the sausage and red pepper flakes will leave you craving for more.

Simple Classic Pizza

Fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves on a lightly toasted crust, served with a touch of authenticity and a taste of Italy.
